ROAD TO THE SUPER

Spin for an MLTP Season and Franchise, then draft your favorite player for your squad. Fill out your team with 2 Offenders, 2 Defenders, and a 5th Ball. Simulate your MLTP season to see if you win the Super!

2 OFF · 2 DEF · 1 5TH BALL 2 franchise rerolls 2 season rerolls 292 team-seasons · S10–S39 7-game season + playoffs

Draft to

Every selectable player references a real season statline from TagPro Reference (Caps, Hold, Prevent, Returns, OSCAR, DSCAR, TSCAR), spanning 29 MLTP seasons (Post S10) across 94 franchises. After each spin, the user may reroll the Franchise or Season (2x each). Each drafted player is assigned a simulated TSCAR, normalized to 300 minutes. 5th Balls provide half the TSCAR as a starting player. Historical teams are simulated, with stronger teams occuring in the playoffs. A player is tagged Offense when their OSCAR is higher than their DSCAR for a selected season season, Defense when DSCAR is higher, and Both when the gap is under 5 points. Any bugs? Contact Baay. Road to the Super is an independent project, not affiliated with TagPro.
