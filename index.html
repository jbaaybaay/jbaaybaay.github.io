Spin for an MLTP season and franchise, then draft the standout from that roster — OFF and DEF players are split by who actually produces more on offense (OSCAR) vs. defense (DSCAR), while a BOTH tag marks two-way players whose OSCAR and DSCAR are nearly even \u2014 they can slot into either side. Your roster

Every roster spot reproduces a real season line from TagPro Reference (Caps, Hold, Prevent, Returns, OSCAR, DSCAR, TSCAR), spanning 29 MLTP seasons (S10–S14, S25, S33, S35–S39, and more) across 58 franchises. Holdin' Gate Warriors leads the pool with 11 seasons, followed by The Land Before Timers (8), The Holy Seehawks (7), Centra of Attention (6), and Boostin Dynamo (5). A player is tagged Offense when their OSCAR is higher than their DSCAR for that season, Defense when DSCAR is higher, and Both when the gap is under 5 points — a rough cut that can change as the data grows.
